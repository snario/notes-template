  \section{Euclidean $n$-space}
  In this section, we will review the algebra of vectors and the structure of the Euclidean $n$-space, $\R^n$. \\ \\
  \textbf{Course overview}
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Sequences, limits, continuity in $\R^n$
  \item Sets and topology of Rn (open, closed, compact, etc.)
  \item Derivatives (requires a good grasp of linear algebra)
  \item Multivariable integrals
  \item Connections between derivatives and integrals
  \end{itemize}
